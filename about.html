What Is Tagtaly?

Every day, 500+ UK news articles flood newsrooms. Editors scramble to understand what's trending, what readers care about, and what competitors are covering. Tagtaly solves this by transforming raw news volume into clear, actionable intelligence—automatically, every morning at 7 AM.

We collect headlines, analyze them for topics and sentiment, detect viral angles, and package it all into shareable visualizations. No manual work. No delays. Just data-driven news intelligence delivered daily.

How It Works

Our pipeline runs in four steps, each designed to surface what matters most in UK news.

Step 1: Data Collection

We aggregate 500+ articles daily from BBC, Guardian, Sky News, and The Independent using RSS feeds. Each article is deduplicated and stored in our database.

  • Sources: 4 major UK news outlets
  • Volume: 500+ articles daily
  • Method: RSS feed aggregation with MD5 deduplication

Step 2: Analysis

We classify each article by topic (Politics, Health, Economy, Crime, Energy, and 5 others) and analyze sentiment using TextBlob NLP. This reveals what editors are covering and how they're framing it.

  • Topics: 9 categories tracked
  • Sentiment: Positive, neutral, or negative
  • Granularity: Article-level and outlet-level analysis

Step 3: Story Detection

We run 5 proprietary algorithms to find the most dramatic news angles—the stories that tell the real story of what's happening.

  • Surge Detection: Topics exploding week-over-week
  • Political Scorecard: Which politicians dominate headlines
  • Record Alerts: Highest/lowest mentions ever
  • Sentiment Shifts: Mood swings by topic
  • Media Bias: What each outlet chooses to cover

Step 4: Visualization

We design 3 charts daily—Instagram-ready, mobile-optimized, bold. Each chart tells a story about what's happening in UK news. Each is ready to post and share.

  • Format: 1080x1350px Instagram portrait
  • Design: Bold, minimal, data-focused
  • Output: 3 charts + social captions, every morning

Data Sources

All data comes from public RSS feeds. We cite all sources. We only use headlines and summaries—no full articles are republished.

BBC News

The UK's national broadcaster. Trusted, comprehensive coverage across politics, business, health, science, and more.

The Guardian

Centre-left national newspaper. Known for investigative journalism and opinion-driven coverage of UK politics and international news.

Sky News

24-hour rolling news. Focuses on breaking news, politics, and real-time updates with a more commercial angle.

The Independent

Digital-first news outlet. Emphasizes political commentary, business coverage, and culture with a centre-left perspective.

Technology Stack

Tagtaly is built with open-source tools and runs entirely on GitHub Actions. The pipeline is transparent, reproducible, and freely available on GitHub.

Core Technologies

  • Python 3.10+: Core pipeline language for data processing
  • SQLite: Historical data storage and article database
  • pandas: Data manipulation and aggregation
  • matplotlib & seaborn: Chart generation and visualization
  • TextBlob: Natural language processing for sentiment analysis
  • GitHub Actions: Daily automation and scheduling
